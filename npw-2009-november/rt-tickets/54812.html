This is an issue within Parrot at the moment, in that named
parameters can always be supplied from positional arguments.
I have a message to parrot-porters requesting clarification,
so will hold off on this ticket until that is resolved.

http://groups.google.com/group/perl.perl6.internals/msg/96417ed37799cd37

If Parrot isn't likely to change here, then we'll have to do some
workarounds involving slurpy arrays and/or slurpy hashes in order
to determine when there are unmatched named parameters.
