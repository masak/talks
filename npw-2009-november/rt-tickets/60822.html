> Lexical subroutines are still todo.

Status update: 'my sub name { ... }' is parsed, but it's not lexical:

$ ./perl6 -e '{ my sub f { say "in f" }; f() }; f(); { my sub f { say
"in second f" }; f() }; f()'
in f
in f
in f
in f

That should die on the second call to f().
