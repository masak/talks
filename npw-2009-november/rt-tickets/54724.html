This appears to stem from problems with 'join' in Str.pir ('reverse' uses 'join'). The following is
test code demonstrating the problem:

my $str = 'Foo';
my @chars = $str.split('');
my $str2 = @chars.join('');
say $str.chars;
say $str2.chars;
